**Badge** — compact status/category marker in mono type, pill-shaped.

```jsx
<Badge tone="ember" dot>Active</Badge>
<Badge tone="up" variant="soft">+312%</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" variant="outline">v2.4</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | ember | up | warn | down | info`. Variants: `soft | outline | solid`. Use `dot` for live/status indicators.
