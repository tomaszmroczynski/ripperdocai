01 The idea

Ripperdoc designs digital augmentations that expand the capabilities of people, teams and organizations.

In the Cyberpunk world, a ripperdoc enhanced the body with implants. Today those implants are AI, automation and knowledge systems — and they enhance the mind. We are not an AI company. We are the specialist who makes one person capable of what once took a team. The human stays the hero; technology is the augmentation.

02 Manifesto

In the past, tools amplified human muscle.
Today, intelligent systems amplify human thought.

A modern ripperdoc does not install cybernetic eyes.
They help people see more through data,
think faster through AI,
and remember through knowledge systems.

If implants enhanced the body,
intelligence enhances the mind.
